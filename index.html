Script

Click on any character's line to highlight all lines spoken by that character.

Hamlet: To be, or not to be: that is the question.

Ophelia: Good my lord, how does your honour for this many a day?

Hamlet: I humbly thank you; well, well, well.

Ophelia: My lord, I have remembrances of yours, that I have longed long to re-deliver.

Claudius: And can you, by no drift of circumstance, get from him why he puts on this confusion?

Hamlet: No, not I; I never gave you aught.

Claudius: Love! his affections do not that way tend.

Ophelia: O, what a noble mind is here o'erthrown!

Hamlet: Get thee to a nunnery.

How It Works

This page uses JavaScript and the DOM to highlight lines spoken by a selected character. Each line contains a data-actor attribute. When a user clicks a line, JavaScript identifies the actor and highlights every line spoken by that actor while removing the highlight from all other characters.
